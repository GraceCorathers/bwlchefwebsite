Sliced Steak

The best steak cooked exactly right and served up with a side of potatoes

Sliced Steak

Ingredients:

  • 2 steaks (your preferred cut)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 bottle of olive oil or stick of butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 baby golden potatoes (chopped in half)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary or thyme (optional)

    • Directions:

    1. Prepare the Potatoes: Preheat your oven to 400°F. Cut the potatoes into wedges or cubes. Toss in olive oil, salt, pepper, and optional garlic powder or dried herbs, then spread them on a baking sheet in a single layer.
    2. Roast the Potatoes: Place the baking sheet in the oven and roast for 25-30 minutes until golden and crispy.
    3. Prepare the Steak: Season the steaks with salt and pepper on both sides
    4. Cook the Steak: Heat olive oil or butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the steaks and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side for medium-rare. Add garlic to the pan for extra flavor.
    5. Rest the Steak: Remove the steaks from the skillet and let them rest on a plate for 5 minutes before cutting.
    6. Serve: Slice and serve the steak alongside the roasted potatoes, and enjoy!

    Teriyaki Chicken

    A healthy and protein packed meal of bite sized chicken pieces and rice served with a delicious teriyaki sauce

    Teriyaki Chicken

    Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (cut into bite-sized pieces)
  • 1 bottle of olive oil
  • 1 bottle of soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 bottle of teriyaki sauce
  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt and pepper

    • Directions:

    1. Prepare the Rice: Bring a medium pot of water to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover. Add rice and simmer for 15 minutes until the rice is cooked.
    2.Cook the Chicken Bites: Heat olive oil and garlic in a large skillet or pan over medium-high heat. Add the cubed chicken pieces in a single layer and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until golden brown and cooked through.
    3. Add the Teriyaki Sauce: Once the chicken is almost fully cooked, pour the teriyaki sauce all over the chicken. Add a splash of soy sauce and salt and pepper to taste. Stir to coat the chicken evenly, and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens and the chicken is well-glazed.
    4. Serve: Fluff the cooked rice with a fork and divide it between plates. Spoon the teriyaki chicken bites over the rice and enjoy!

    Turkey Sandwich

    With a twist on the traditional club sandwich, this revamped recipe will be best turkey sandwich you'll ever have

    Turkey Sandwich

    Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of sliced deli turkey
  • 2 pieces of sourdough bread
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 bottle of mayo
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 apple
  • 1 lettuce
  • 1 red onion

    • Directions:

    1. Toast the Bread: Place the two slices of sourdough bread into the toaster on the setting you desire
    2. Build the Sandwich: First spread the mayo onto the toasted bread. Next, add the turkey, sliced tomato, sliced onion and sliced apple on top.
    3. Serve: Slice in half and enjoy!

    Buttered Noodles

    Perfect for a comfort meal and a cozy night in, these butter noodles with veggies can ease any mood

    Buttered Noodles

    Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of frozen veggies (peas, carrots, and corn)
  • 1 box of fusili pasta (or preferred shape)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 stick of butter or dairy free butter alternative
  • 1 cup of shrredded cheese or dairy free alternative
  • 1 container of minced garlic
  • 1/4 cup of water

    • Directions:

    1. Start the Pasta: Boil a medium pot of water and pour the box pasta in once ready. Cook 9 minutes or until al dente.
    2. Begin with the Veggies: Pour frozen veggies into a medium sauce pan and steam with a 1/4 cup of water
    3. Sautee Veggies : Once the water has evaporated and the veggies are no longer frozen, add in butter, salt, pepper and minced garlic to the pan and sautee until incoperated.
    4. Mix: Once the pasta is cooked, preserve 1/2 cup of pasta water and drain the rest of water out. Place the pasta back into the pot and pour back in the pasta water. Add in the veggie mixture and shrredded cheese.
    5. Serve: Mix throughly in the pot and serve into a bowl. Add extra cheese on top for a cozy dinner!
